The selection of appropriate data and its proper preparation are crucial for the effectiveness of a machine learning model. Challenges in machine learning, such as overfitting, underfitting, and insufficient or inadequate data, present significant issues that can affect the accuracy and reliability of models. For a model to effectively learn patterns and relationships, it requires a large amount of high-quality data. If the data is insufficient, incomplete, or improperly prepared, the quality of the model will be limited. A lack of diversity in the data can also lead to incorrect conclusions and an incomplete understanding of real-world relationships, which negatively impacts the model's performance.
